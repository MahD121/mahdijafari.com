Biography

Mahdi Jafari is an innovative architect and computational designer based in Kabul and Stuttgart. With diverse experience in architecture, he aims to merge cutting-edge technology and artistic vision by incorporating computational morphologies into design. Recognized for his exceptional talent during his bachelor's studies, where he won a prestigious competition for hillside design, Mahdi is poised to make a lasting impact on the architectural landscape through his commitment to excellence and holistic problem-solving.
